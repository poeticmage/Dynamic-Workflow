3. Conduct technical interview:

5. Score candidate responses:
   - 10-point scale per question; follow-up out of 5.
   - Rubric: correctness, depth, clarity, experience.

6. Generate final evaluation report:
   - Combined job requirements, resume summary, scored responses.
   - Calculated overall score out of 100.
